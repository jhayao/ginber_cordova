Payment & Order Questions

At HammerTime Hardware, we accept several convenient payment methods: Cash on Delivery (COD) for local orders, GCash for instant digital payments, and bank transfers to our BPI or BDO accounts. We also accept major credit and debit cards. Choose the payment method that works best for you during checkout.

Once your payment is processed, you'll receive an email confirmation within 15-30 minutes. For GCash and bank transfers, please keep your transaction reference number and send it to us via Facebook messenger or WhatsApp. You can also check your order status by logging into your account on our website.

Currently, we don't offer gift cards at HammerTime Hardware. However, we do provide flexible payment options including installment plans for larger purchases. Contact our customer service team to discuss special arrangements for bulk orders or corporate accounts.

You can track your order by logging into your account on our website and viewing your order history. For local deliveries within Oroquieta City, we'll provide updates via SMS or call. For orders shipped via LBC or J&T Express, you'll receive a tracking number to monitor your package's progress directly on their websites.

If you're having trouble with payment, first check your internet connection and card details. For GCash issues, ensure you have sufficient balance. If problems persist, contact our customer service at 0917-123-4567 or visit our store at Oroquieta City, Misamis Occidental. We're here to help resolve any payment difficulties quickly.

If your order is delayed beyond the expected delivery time, please contact us immediately at 0917-123-4567. Local deliveries within Oroquieta City typically arrive within 1-2 business days. For shipped orders, delivery may take 3-7 business days depending on your location. We'll investigate any delays and provide updates on your order status.

About Us

HammerTime Hardware is a proudly homegrown hardware retail company based in the heart of Oroquieta City, Misamis Occidental. Known for its bold identity and strong commitment to customer satisfaction, HammerTime Hardware offers a wide range of tools, construction materials, plumbing and electrical supplies, paint, fasteners, and home improvement essentials.
